About our salon

Our team of dedicated professionals, with a wealth of experience and expertise, uses the latest techniques and products to provide you with the best results. Each treatment in our salon is carefully designed to meet the specific needs of our clients, whether you want skin revitalization, deep relaxation or just a moment of rest from everyday life.
Luminique is more than a beauty salon – it's a community based on trust, quality and commitment to your well-being.
Our mission is to inspire every client to feel beautiful, confident and happy in their own skin. At Luminique, we believe that beauty goes beyond the surface. Our serene, welcoming environment is thoughtfully designed to offer a sanctuary where you can recharge and reconnect with yourself.

Our vision

Our vision at Luminique Salon is to become a place where everyone can find a moment of peace and refreshment, with the opportunity to express themselves through beauty. We aim to enable you to shine and feel your best in your own skin through superior service and a careful approach to each client.
At Luminique, we believe that beauty is not only external, but also a sense of inner satisfaction and confidence. That's why we try so that every client feels special attention and caring care that exceeds expectations. Our goal is to create a space where you will always feel welcome, where we inspire you to embrace your uniqueness and bring out the best in you.

Our mission

The mission of Luminique salon is simple but powerful: we are dedicated to raising your confidence and taking care of your skin, hair and appearance with the latest methods and high quality products. We approach each treatment with love, professionalism and dedication, believing that beauty lies in the details and an individual approach that provides the best results.
Our mission is to create an oasis where every client feels unique and special. In addition to following global trends and perfecting techniques, in the Luminique salon we foster a personalized approach, because we believe that the beauty of each person deserves unique attention. Through carefully designed treatments, we aim to inspire and encourage our clients to always be the best version of themselves.
